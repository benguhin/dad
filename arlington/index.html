Details for Arlington Burial & Celebration of Life

We look forward to seeing you!



Wednesday Evening, August 31st

Welcome / Meet and Greet from 6pm - 10pm

Sheraton Pentagon City Hospitality Room - Stars, 900 S Orme St

  • Bring your own food- we will also have pizza and silverware will be provided.
  • Per hotel rules- please don't bring alcohol, there will be a cash bar
  • This is a way to see each other before the next morning funeral


Thursday Morning, September 1st

Lt Col Joseph Guhin Burial at Arlington Cemetery, 9am

Arlington National Cemetery, 1 Memorial Ave, Fort Myer, VA 22211

This will be a full in-ground burial with USAF honors and includes band, casket team, bugler, caisson, escort and firing party.

  • REQUIRED to arrive no later than 8:15am (45 min ahead of the 9am starting time) at the Administrative Office Building.
  • 100% ID check for those over 16. No alcohol or firearms. Please see this fact sheet for more information on security and entrance requirements or visit arlingtoncemetery.mil/visit/security.
  • If driving, tell security you're there for a service. Your car will be directed to a queuing lane specifically for Joe's service, which will be led to the ceremony.
  • If you are arriving without a car, you will need to join another guest's vehicle for the procession to the gravesite. Make arrangements beforehand to make sure there is room in another vehicle. Most attendees are on the list to take the bus, which is now full.
  • 10 to 15 minutes before the service, our procession will depart from the queuing lanes to the gravesite location.
  • BUS PICK UP at the Sheraton Pentagon City for those needing ride, LEAVES hotel at 7:45 am. It will be a full bus so please make sure you are there in time. Bus will stay with riders through funeral and return back to the hotel around 10:45am.
  • Arlington National Cemetery suggests wearing attire that is appropriate for the season, but also respects the dignity of the occasion. Proper footwear that allows mobility on hard and soft surfaces is recommended.


Thursday Evening, September 1st

Celebration of Joe Guhin, 5:30 pm

Sheraton Pentagon City Galaxy Ballroom, 900 S Orme St

  • Please be there at 5:30 pm to get your nametag, find your table, and enjoy the open bar.
  • Dinner will begin at 6:00 pm. This will be a Chop House dinner buffet for adults as well as a children's buffet.
    • Open Bar from 5:30 pm until 8:30 pm, featuring Joe's favorite Margaritas as well as beer, wine and other cocktails.
    • Cash Bar from 8:30 pm to 11:30 pm.
  • After dinner and before dessert, there will be a speech and video presentation followed by dancing to celebrate Joe being in our lives. Please stay and enjoy each other's company and share Joe stories.
  • In honor of Joe and his crazy sweet tooth, there will be a candy bar available throughout the evening and an icecream sundae bar along with two other desserts that will be served after the video presentation.
  • Dress for a party.
