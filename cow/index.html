The Cow and the Elephant

The cow and the elephant
and the dog
went down the river
on a great big log.
The cow said MOO,
and the dog said RUFF,
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the sheep
went down the river
on an army jeep.
The cow said MOO
and the sheep said BAA
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the bear
went down the river
on a rocking chair.
The cow said MOO
and the bear said GRRR
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the goose
went down the river
on a red caboose.
The cow said MOO
and the goose said HONK
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the crow
went down the river
on an old banjo.
The cow said MOO
and the crow said CAW
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the cat
went down the river
on a fireman’s hat.
The cow said MOO
and the cat said MEOW
and the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the owl
went down the river
on a garden trowel
The cow said moo, and the owl said hoo,
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the snake
went down the river
on a birthday cake.
The cow said MOO
and the snake said HISS
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the goat
went down the river
on a root-beer float.
The cow said MOO
and the goat said MAA
and the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and the mouse
went down the river
on a cape cod house.
The cow said MOO
and the mouse said SQUEAK
And the elephant sang along, TRA LA!

The cow and the elephant
and their gang
sailed into space
on a boomerang.
The cow said, “Oooh,
What a lovely view!”
And everyone sang along, TRA LA!
